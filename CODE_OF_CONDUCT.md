# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ArthemizLabs is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors, maintainers, and participants across its repositories, issue trackers, and communication channels.

We expect all participants to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with professional standards in a production engineering environment.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and respectfully, regardless of role or experience level.
- Provide constructive, specific, and actionable feedback in code reviews and issue discussions.
- Accept feedback and criticism of work without taking it personally.
- Acknowledge the contributions of others and give appropriate credit.
- Prioritize the technical and organizational goals of the project over personal preferences.
- Raise disagreements through appropriate channels — issue discussions, architectural review, or direct communication — not through public disputes.
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive technical, business, or security information.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks in any form.
- Discriminatory language or conduct based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, nationality, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
- Dismissive, condescending, or demeaning communication.
- Publishing private information about another person without their explicit consent.
- Deliberate sabotage of work, infrastructure, or processes.
- Submitting malicious code, backdoors, or security vulnerabilities intentionally.
- Repeated disregard for established engineering processes after being informed of them.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by ArthemizLabs, including:

- All public and private GitHub repositories under the ArthemizLabs organization
- Issue trackers, pull request discussions, and code review threads
- Any communication channels (official chat, video calls, or mailing lists) associated with ArthemizLabs projects
- In-person or virtual events where individuals represent ArthemizLabs

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the organization maintainers through the contact information listed in the affected repository or via the organization profile.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and with appropriate discretion. The maintainers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of the reporter.

**Consequences for violations may include:**

- A private written warning with required corrective action
- Temporary suspension of contribution privileges
- Permanent removal from the project and organization

Enforcement decisions are made at the discretion of the organization maintainers and are final.
